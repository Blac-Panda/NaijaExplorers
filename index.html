    Who We Are:

    We a group of drongrapher who love riding bicycles. We travel across Nigeria and bring some of the most beautiful sites every summer and spring. You can also join in on the tour (only 5 spots).

    Tour Details

    Every year, we open up 3 spots for our summer explorer tour. Three selected people get to travel along side the Explore Naija Team, all expenses paid, courtesy of Views from Naija Platform.

    • Guided Trips

      You get to explorer uncharted tracks with some of the most thrilling fun seeker you can ever find. Don't worry, you won't get lost :) .

    • Photo Trips

      Want to experience nature's beauty ? You'll get to ride with the bicycle squad, and if you don't want the execise, you join the bus team.

    • Biking Trips

      If you really love cycling, consider taking a tour through one of our mountain bike paths. We'll provide the bikes, and lunch too!

    • Racing Trips

      Got a competitive spirit? Sign up for one of our mountain marathons! Try to reach the summit before anyone else.
